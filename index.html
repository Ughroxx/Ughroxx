ABOUT UGHROXX
Ughroxx arises to represent myself, you, and all those who fear being average.
It's not just a brand, it's an attitude towards life. A rebellion against sameness. A space for those who crave meaning, raw expression, and the courage to stand out.
Ughroxx is where fashion meets art. My mind doesn't work only as a designer, but as an artist, obsessed with pushing boundaries, telling stories, and creating pieces that make people feel. Every design reflects a journey.
Every piece is 100% vegan-friendly, because making art should never come at the cost of another life.
Ughroxx is for those who want more, from themselves, from art, from life.
This is more than style.
This is a mindset.
This is Ughroxx.
